BritSync AI Chatbot

Your website's intelligent concierge, powered by local Ollama or high-speed Groq Cloud.

How to Embed

Copy and paste this script before the closing </body> tag of your website.

<script src="https://britsync-assistant.netlify.app/widget.js"></script>
<script>
  BritSyncChat.init({
    provider: 'ollama',
    model: 'llama3'
  });
</script>
