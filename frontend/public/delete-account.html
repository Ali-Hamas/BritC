Account Deletion

Delete Your Account

Last updated: April 25, 2026

If you no longer wish to use BritSync AI (also known as Britsee), you can request that we permanently delete your account and all data associated with it. This page explains how.

App and Developer

  • App name: BritSync AI (Britsee)
  • Developer: BritSync
  • Website: https://britsyncai.com

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your BritSync AI account, send an email from the email address registered to your account to:

info@britsyncai.com

Use the subject line: "Delete My Account" and include the following in the body:

  1. Your account email address
  2. A brief confirmation that you want your account permanently deleted

We will verify your request and complete the deletion within 14 days. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

Data TypeAction
Account email and password (hashed)Deleted
Business profile (name, industry, audience, goals)Deleted
Team data (members, roles, invitations)Deleted
Active sessions and authentication tokensDeleted
Chat history stored locally on your deviceCleared from your device when you uninstall the app or sign out

What Data Is Kept

For legal, security, and fraud-prevention reasons we may retain a small subset of data after account deletion:

  • Anonymized usage analytics that cannot be linked back to you (retention: up to 24 months)
  • Records required to comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax, anti-fraud) — retained only as long as legally required
  • Backup snapshots may contain residual data for up to 30 days before being permanently overwritten

Clearing Local Chat History

Chat history is stored locally in your browser or app storage on the device you use BritSync AI from. You can clear it by:

  • On the website: sign out, then clear your browser's site data for britsyncai.com
  • On Android: uninstall the app, or go to Settings → Apps → BritSync AI → Storage → Clear Data

Need Help?

If you have any questions about deletion, your data, or this process, contact us at info@britsyncai.com. We typically respond within 1–2 business days.
