Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BritSync ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the BritSync AI mobile application and website (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: email address, name, and password (stored securely as a hash) when you create an account.
  • Business profile data: business name, industry, audience, and revenue goals that you voluntarily provide.
  • Chat content: messages you send to Britsync AI. These are processed to generate responses and may be stored locally on your device for your chat history.
  • Team data: team members and roles you invite or create.
  • Technical data: IP address, device type, browser type, and basic usage analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service, including authentication and personalized AI responses.
  • To send transactional emails such as password resets.
  • To improve and secure the Service.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate BritSync AI:

  • Groq, OpenRouter: AI model providers that process your chat messages to generate responses.
  • Supabase: database hosting for account and profile data.
  • Resend: transactional email delivery (password resets).
  • Better-Auth: authentication and session management.

These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy and security policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Chat history is stored locally on your device using your browser or app's local storage.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only:

  • With the third-party services listed above, strictly to operate the app.
  • When required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights.
  • With your explicit consent.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.
  • Export your business profile and chat history.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@britsyncai.com.

7. Children's Privacy

BritSync AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain account and profile data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time. Chat history stored locally on your device is under your control and can be cleared from the app at any time.

9. International Users

Your information may be processed in countries outside your own. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers. We take reasonable steps to ensure your data is treated securely regardless of location.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the app or email. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at:
